Our Story

FOUNDED ON
PRECISION

Under the visionary leadership of Managing Director Mr. M. Ravi Kumar, Sri Asha Forgings Pvt. Ltd. was established to push the boundaries of metallurgical engineering. Starting with a commitment to excellence, we have grown into a leading name in specialised aluminium alloy forgings.

Our journey is defined by continuous technical innovation, from mastering complex aerospace geometries to pioneering 2195 Aluminium Lithium alloy forging in India. We operate with a philosophy that precision is not just a requirement, but our legacy.
