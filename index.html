Our Story

THE SRI ASHA
FORGING STORY

Sri Asha Forgings Pvt. Ltd. is a specialised manufacturer of precision aluminium alloy components since 2004. We focus on metallurgical excellence and engineering rigour, delivering zero-defect forged solutions for aerospace and defence sectors.

Our core capabilities include drop-bottom heat treatment, 2195 Lithium alloy forging, and precision multi-axis machining — providing a vertically integrated solution for high-performance industrial components and drawing-specific prototypes.

Our Facility

OUR INFRASTRUCTURE

Sri Asha Forgings operates a facility designed for high-performance aluminium alloy forgings with precision. Our infrastructure combines robust forging hammers with modern drop-bottom furnaces and precision machining capability.

The facility is equipped with heavy-duty open die forging hammers and specialised machining systems allowing accurate finishing of products including rings, shafts, and specialised industrial components.
